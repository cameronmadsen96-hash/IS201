Fun Facts About the Braves

  • The Braves are the oldest continuously operating professional sports franchise in America.
  • When the Braves won the World Series in 2021, none of their Opening Day starting outfielders played in the World Series.
  • The Braves currently have the largest divisional lead across all of Major League Baseball.
  • Hank Aaron hit 755 career homeruns. Even if he never hit a homer, he would still have over 3,000 career hits.
  • Ronald Acuña Jr. is the only player in Baseball history with 40+ homeruns and 70+ stolen bases in a single season.
  • The Braves franchise record for team runs scored in a single game is 29, which was set in 2020 against the Miami Marlins.
